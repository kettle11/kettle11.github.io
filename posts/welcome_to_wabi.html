Welcome to Wabi!

Wabi is a work in progress. Check back later to learn more!

To update a website with Wabi:

  • Edit a post, page, or website design file on your computer.
  • Type "publish" into wabi and press Enter.
  • Your website is online for the world to enjoy!

Wabi is designed to be incredibly simple without compromising your vision. You can learn Wabi in seconds and use it to make any form of static website.

Things I'm working on right now for Wabi:

  • Pagination

  • General code cleanup
