Microsoft HoloLens


I worked on the HoloLens at Microsoft where I was responsible for a variety of front-end components related to the HoloLens "shell". I was involved in various UI / UX prototyping efforts working with the HoloLens design, prototyping, product management, and engineering teams. I also worked on the first generation of Microsoft Mixed Reality headsets and helped develop the Mixed Reality shell for the platform.


PhotoSynth 2

I did two summer internships with the Photosynth team at Microsoft. During my first internship I prototyped the user experience and interaction for a 3D annotations feature for photogammetry data. This feature shipped as part of "Photosynth 2" and enabled a rich community of annoted photogammetry.

During my second internship I developed and prototyped interactions for a secretive emerging AR platform within Microsoft that later was revealed to be the HoloLens.


Moxi Music

As a personal project I set out to make the best music app for Windows Phone and Windows. The goal was to create something that was powerful and aesthetically pleasing. I wanted to bring the polish of apps in the Apple ecosystem to a Windows app. A core feature of Moxi was integration into OneDrive, providing the advantages of cloud services and the strengths of a native app.


Color Bird

When looking at a sunset I wanted to extract a gradient to use in a game I was working on. A few others and I created "Color Bird" to gather the primary colors from regions of photos. A user could simply swipe in a circle around an area and Color Bird would give them the colors from that area. The app was released on Android for the Google Play store.


Kajo

Kajo is a first person platforming adventure. The game evokes a feeling of wonder and was designed to encourage mastery of game mechanics and levels through repeated playthroughs. It was created as a student project and accepted to Steam Greenlight (back when that was an exclusive thing). I created the initial concept and designed/developed the gameplay along with a team of other students.
